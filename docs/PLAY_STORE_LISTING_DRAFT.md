## Full Description (4000 characters max)

FoodFit Scan helps you understand what's in your packaged food before you buy it. Point the camera at a barcode, and instantly see the product's nutrition information, allergen data, additives, and processing level — all checked against your personal preferences.

**Suitability scoring**
Tell FoodFit Scan which allergens to watch for, whether you want to avoid ultra-processed foods, and your personal nutrition caps (sugar, salt, saturated fat). Every product you scan gets a clear suitability rating: Good match, Caution, or Avoid — with plain-English reasons.

**Allergen awareness**
Allergen data is shown prominently for every product, including allergens, traces, and food additives. Always check the packaging — food data may be incomplete.

**Healthier alternatives**
When a product is flagged as Caution or Avoid, FoodFit Scan can suggest alternatives in the same food category with better suitability for your preferences.

**Scan history**
Your recent scans are saved locally so you can revisit products without scanning again. Tap any item to go straight to its detail page.

**Private by design**
No account required. Your preferences, scan history, and cached product data are all stored on your device. The only data sent over the internet is the barcode number, used to look up product information from Open Food Facts.

**Powered by Open Food Facts**
Product data is provided by Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org), a free and open food products database. Data may be incomplete or inaccurate. Always check the product packaging, especially for allergens.

_FoodFit Scan is an informational food label helper, not medical or dietary advice. Do not rely on this app to manage allergies, medical conditions, or dietary treatments._
